Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about consultations, demonstrations, and purchases.

What is a MedBed consultation?
A one‑on‑one discussion to understand your goals, explain device options, and outline next steps such as demonstrations, sessions, or purchasing.

How do I book an appointment?
Use the Schedule Consultation button to select a time. You’ll receive a confirmation and a secure link for your session.

Are demonstrations available?
Yes. Private demonstrations can be arranged during or after your consultation, subject to availability.

Can I purchase a unit?
Absolutely. After a consultation and optional demo, our team will provide a formal quotation and secure checkout process.

Do you provide support after purchase?
Yes. We offer onboarding, documentation, and ongoing client support, including maintenance scheduling and assistance.
